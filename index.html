Welcome to Arena Animation Mangalore - Celebrating 25 Years of Excellence in Animation Education!

For a quarter of a century, Arena Animation Mangalore has been at the forefront of shaping the future of animation, visual effects, gaming, web and graphics, broadcast, digital design & marketing, and multimedia education in India. Our journey, which began in 1996, has been marked by unwavering dedication to providing top-notch training and empowering creative minds to excel in the dynamic world of multimedia and entertainment.

With a legacy that spans two and a half decades, Arena Animation Mangalore has proudly educated and nurtured over 4,50,000 students across 20 countries. Our commitment to quality education and cutting-edge curriculum has consistently set us apart as a leader in the field.
